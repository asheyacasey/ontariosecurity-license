Why the next module is still locked?

The Ministry requires that students spend a minimum amount of time studying each module. You cannot go to the next module until both of the following conditions are satisfied:

  • the module quiz has been completed (if applicable - some modules may not have an associated quiz), and
  • the module timer has expired

When both requirements are satisfied, you get access to the next module.

If you've worked through the module, completed the quiz faster and you have lots of time left over, then please take a break, get a coffee, go for a walk etc., and then return and revise the material until the next module unlocks.

Studies show the human brain learns and retains information better when taking breaks in between learning sessions.
